Important Questions & Answers


Q1. Combine terms: 12a + 26b -4b – 16a.


Sol => 12a + 26b -4b – 16a.
= 12a – 16a + 26b – 4b.
= -4a + 22b


Q2. Simplify: (4 – 5) – (13 – 18 + 2).


Sol => (4 – 5) – (13 – 18 + 2).
= -1-(13+2-18).
= -1-(15-18).
= -1-(-3).
= -1+3.
= 2.


Q3. Factor: 5x2 – 15x – 20


Sol => 5x2 – 15x – 20.
= 5(x2 – 3x – 4).
= 5(x2 – 4x + x – 4).
= 5{x(x - 4) +1(x - 4)}.
= 5(x-4)(x+1).
